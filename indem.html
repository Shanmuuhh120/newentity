Salon Organizer App

Appointment Customer Reward

Appointment

Purpose: Represents an appointment for a customer at the salon.

Attributes:
ID: Unique identifier for the appointment.
Service Name: Name of the service the customer is booking.
Appointment Time: Date and time of the scheduled appointment.
Status: Current status (Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled).
Customer: Many-to-one relationship with Customer.

Customer

Purpose: Represents a customer who books salon services.

Attributes:
ID: Unique identifier for the customer.
Username: The customer's username (for login).
Email: The customer's email address.
Password: Stored securely.
Appointments: One-to-many relationship with Appointment.
Reward: One-to-one relationship with Reward.

Reward

Purpose: Represents the rewards system for customers.

Attributes:
ID: Unique identifier for the reward.
Points: Number of reward points earned.
Customer: One-to-one relationship with Customer.
Name: Name of the reward (e.g., "VIP Reward", "Bronze Membership").
